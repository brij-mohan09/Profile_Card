Anubhav Dubey Profile

Anubhav Dubey

Anubhav Dubey is an Indian Entrepreneur who hails from Rewa, Madhya Pradesh. He is known for his successful tea business, Chai Sutta Bar, which he launched in 2016 with his friend Anand Nayak in Indore. Anubhav and his friend has managed to turn his company into a booming success with an annual turnover of over 100 crores.
